Welcome to McApp, a financial budgeting assistant.

Your budgeting assistant will help you be confident in your money management plans.

Key Features

  • View all added account balances.
  • Add expenses to compare to current balances.
  • Add sources of income, such as:
    • Employment
    • Investment
    • Real Estate
    • Other
  • Categorize your income sources as:
    • Daily
    • Weekly
    • Bi-weekly
    • Monthly
    • Quarterly
    • Yearly
